2nd Screen Media&Commerce / Media and commerce service

Discipline: Professional Concept

Discipline: Professional Concept

The information on the TV screen is presented in the form of a minimal card to minimize interference between information. The card expands as users zoom in on a particular piece of information, and this interaction enhances users’ focus. Also, retrieving third-party CP contents is as easy as taking a card from a wallet, enabling users to consume various contents quickly.

  • TARGET REGIONS Asia, Australia/Oceania, Europe, North America, South America
